The initial view shows a radar and a legend with a list of technologies that appear in the radar.

An empty radar just shows the list of 4 quadrants.

The legend has an abbreviated name that serves as a key and appears on top of the radar blip for that technology on the radar.

Entries in the legend are grouped by quadrant and sorted closest to center to farthest.

Technology names and legend keys are not guaranteed to be unique. Users must edit the names if they want them to be unique.

Click on the title, quadrant name, or technology name to edit it.

Click on an existing target on the radar and drag to relocate it.

Click anywhere on the radar to create a new technology entry.

  • It will appear with a default name, in the correct place in the legend.
  • The name can be changed by clicking on it in the legend.
