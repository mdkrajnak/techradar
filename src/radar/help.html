Start Up

The first time the application displays an empty radar. Following start ups it displays the last opened or saved radar.

Display

In the center of the window is a 4 quadrant radar display. Across the top of the radar is the title and on the right is the open menu double "<<" indicator.

Next to each quadrant is a legend listing the technologies in that quadrant. The legend gives an abbreviation that identifies the technology and the technology name. The legend entries are sorted by the distance from the center of the radar. Items at the top are closest to the center. NOTE! The application does not prevent entering the same technology name more than once.

Editing

Add:Click anywhere on the radar display to create a new technology entry.

Rename: New entries are automatically named "New Tech". Click on the name in the legend to edit the technology name. The technology key will be automatically updated.

Titles: Click directly on the legend title or the radar title to edit the text.

Move: Click on the blip shown on the radar and drag to reposition. The legend will update dynamically.

Delete: Hover over a entry's bullet when it turns into the indicator click on the X and the item will be deleted.

Menu

Click on the open menu "<<" indicator to slide the menu form the right. Click the "Open...", "Save...", "Print...", or "Help..." menu items to activate the desired actions. NOTE! Currently to get a 1 page print make sure you set the page orientation to "Landscape" and set the margins to "None". Use the close menu ">>" control to close the menu.
