Start Up

On startup the application will display the last opened or saved radar. If none, then an empty radar display is shown.

Display

In the center of the window is a 4 quadrent radar display.

Adjacent to each quadrent is legend listing the technologies in that quadrent. The legend gives a key, an abbrevated string that identifies the technology, and the technology name. NOTE! The application does not prevent users from entering the same technology name more than once.

The entries in the legend are sorted by the distance from the center of the radar. Items at the top are closest to the center.

Across the top of the radar is the radar title and on the right is the open menu double "<<" indicator

Editing

Add:Click anywhere on the radar display to create a new technology entry.

Rename: New entries are automatically named "New Tech". Click on the name in the legend to edit the technology name. The technology key will be automatically updated.

Titles: Click directly on the legend title or the radar title to edit the text in place.

Move: Click on directly on the technology blip shown on the radar and drag to reposition the blip. The legend updates as the entry is dragged.

Delete: Hover over a entry's list bullet when it turns into the delete indicator click on the X and the item will be deleted.

Menu

Click on the open menu "<<" indicator to slide the menu form the right. Click the "Open...", "Save...", "Print...", or "Help..." menu items to activate the desired actions. NOTE! Currently to get a 1 page print make sure you set the page orientation to "Landscape" and set the page margins to "None". Use the close menu ">>" control to close the menu.
